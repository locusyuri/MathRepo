\chapter{Fundamental Group} % 基本群

\begin{definition}{Simply Connected Space}\label{def:simply_connected_space} % 单连通空间
    Let \(X\) be a topological space.
    We say \(X\) is \textbf{simply connected} if it is 
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item path-connected, and 
        \item every loop in \(X\) can be continuously contracted to a point, i.e., 
            for any loop \(\gamma: S^1 \to X\), there exists a homotopy \(H: S^1 \times [0, 1] \to X\) such that 
            \(H(s, 0) = \gamma(s)\) for all \(s \in S^1\) and \(H(s, 1) = x_0\) for some fixed point \(x_0 \in X\).
    \end{enumerate}

    % 以上为一般拓扑学中的定义，下面我们用群来刻画单连通空间
    Equivalently, \(X\) is simply connected if its 
    fundamental group \(\pi_1(X, x_0)\) is trivial for some (and hence any) base point \(x_0 \in X\).
    % \(\pi_1(X, x_0)\) 是平凡群也常常表述为 \(\pi_1(X, x_0) = 0\)
    \(\pi_1(X, x_0) = 0\) is also a common way to express that the fundamental group is trivial.
\end{definition}
% 在复分析中, 单连通空间往往被表述为: D 是单连通的当且仅当 D 中任意简单闭曲线的内部完全包含在 D 中, 这也是单连通空间的一个重要性质
In complex analysis, simply connected spaces (\(\in \mathbb{C}\)) are often characterized by the property that 
any simple closed curve in the space has its interior completely contained within the space. 
This is an important property of simply connected spaces.
